1.5 lb potatoes
1 cup olive oil
	stir fry half cooked - 7 minutes
	strain extra oil
	
1 onion thin sliced
6 eggs
2 tbsp broth
	fry in in 2 tbsp oil
	add potatoes on top
	stir, simmer 8 min
	flip & cook for 4 min.

Alternative:
pepper sauteed 
   375 °F, 15 min



Potato Tortilla
Spanish omelet		

1.5 lb potatoes
1 cup olive oil
    half fried - 7 min.
    strain extra oil

1 onion thin sliced
6 eggs
2 tbsp broth
    fry in in 2 tbsp oil
    add potatoes on top
    stir, simmer 8 min
    flip & cook for 4 min.

Alternative:
pepper sauteed 
   375 °F, 15 min
