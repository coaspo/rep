Potato Tortilla
Spanish omelet		Scalloped potatoes
1.5 lb potatoes
1 cup olive oil
    half fry - 7 min.
    strain extra oil

1 onion thin sliced
6 eggs
2 tbsp broth
    fry in 2 tbsp oil
    add potatoes on top
    stir, simmer 8 min
    flip, cook 4 min.

Alternative:
pepper sauteed 
   375 °F, 15 min
2 lb golden potatoes
  cut slices with skin
  put in greased pan

1/2 cup onion
3   tbs butter or oil
1/2     minced garlic
   saute; when done, add:

1/2 tbsp flower
   lower heat, add:
3/4 cup cream or milk 
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup shredder cheddar
    pour on potatoes, & add:
1/2 cup shredder cheddar

Cover & bake: 
   350 °F, 1 hour
